Brahmani Technocast

Brahmani Technocast specializes in high-quality CI (Cast Iron) and SG (Spheroidal Graphite) castings. Our commitment to precision and innovation ensures we deliver exceptional casting solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of our clients across diverse industries.

Brahmani Technocast is a first-generation foundry specializing in high-quality grey cast iron products. With a commitment to transparency and innovation, we deliver casting solutions tailored to your needs. At the heart of our operations is a fully automated, state-of-the-art facility designed to eliminate inconsistencies and ensure precision in every casting. Unlike traditional methods, we rely entirely on machine molding to deliver products that stand the test of time and meet exact specifications.

Our Mission

To deliver precision cast iron and ductile iron products with complete transparency, leveraging cutting-edge technology and innovation to meet and exceed customer expectations.

Our Vision

To become a trusted leader in the foundry industry by setting new benchmarks in quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction, while fostering sustainable growth.
